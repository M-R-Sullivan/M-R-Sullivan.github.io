Price scores are derived from Wine-Searcher average retail prices (USD, ex-tax) for each producer's flagship bottling, mapped via a logarithmic function: Score = 0.926 × ln(price) + 0.7, capped at 10. The reference price is shown in each producer's detail panel. Critics, Reputation, Terroir, and Consistency scores reflect editorial judgement drawing on Meadows, Morris, Kelley, and Robinson. Scale (1–5) measures production volume and does not enter the composite. Style tags acknowledge most estates resist neat categorisation. Rankings are computed by composite score with reputation as tiebreaker. Tap any producer for the full radar chart and data.
